about us
Funraise LLC. was founded by Jack Botein, a high school student looking for how to make fundraising truly fun. Funraise partners with local experiental businesses to host fundraising events during off peak times. 20% of the sale price goes to the cause of the Funraiser.
Why you should list with us
Our fee of 5% is significantly lower than comparable platforms such as groupon (25%), meaning that listing on Funraise is a great value proposition. Further, our customers are likely to return to your business, driving future traffic. Listing with Funraise Boston is easy: just text 555-555-5555 LISTTODAY, and then enter your business name, experience, and when you want to list. No committment, only a 5% fee when we make a sale (we'll text you).
fees and Sales
We charge listing businesses a 5% fee for every sucessful sale. If we process a $20 rock climbing pass, we'll automatically deduct the 5% fee ($1) and the 20% charitable contribution. We'll handle all of the backend work, all you need to do is accept the payment and user pass on the event day.
